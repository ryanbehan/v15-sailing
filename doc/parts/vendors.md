# Vanguard 15 Parts Vendors

Finding parts for the Vanguard 15 can be challenging since the boat is no longer in production. However, several dedicated sailing retailers continue to support the class with compatible parts, sails, and custom rigging.

## Sails
*   **[North Sails](https://northsails.com/sailing/en/od/vanguard-15):** The official manufacturer of class-legal Vanguard 15 sails.
*   **[Quantum Sails](https://www.quantumsails.com/):** Custom sail options.
*   **[Neil Pryde](https://www.neilprydesails.com/):** Performance sails.
*   **[West Coast Sailing](https://westcoastsailing.net/boat-parts/vanguard-15/sails/):** Retailer for V15 sails and accessories.

## Parts & Hardware
*   **[West Coast Sailing](https://westcoastsailing.net/boat-parts/vanguard-15/):** Extensive inventory of V15 parts, sails, and rigging. They offer a wide range of replacement parts, including standing rigging, running rigging, and hull hardware.
*   **[Colie Sails](https://coliesail.com/boats/vanguard-15.html):** Specialized V15 parts and expert advice. Known for their custom spliced lines and knowledgeable staff.
*   **[Intensity Sails](https://www.intensitysails.com/vanguard-15-parts.html):** Cost-effective parts and foils. A great source for affordable replacement rudders, daggerboards, and covers.
*   **[Vela Sailing Supply](https://www.velasailingsupply.com/):** General sailing hardware and rigging.

## Manufacturer
*   **LaserPerformance:** The original manufacturer (Vanguard 15 is now out of production). Parts diagrams are available for reference.
