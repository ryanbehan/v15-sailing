## Sails

Vanguard 15 sails are one-design.

- **Mainsail**: Dacron
- **Jib**: Dacron

## Vendors
*   **[North Sails](https://northsails.com/sailing/en/od/vanguard-15):** The official manufacturer of class-legal Vanguard 15 sails.
*   **[West Coast Sailing](https://westcoastsailing.net/boat-parts/vanguard-15/sails/):** Retailer for V15 sails and accessories.
*   **[Quantum Sails](https://www.quantumsails.com/):** Custom sail options.
*   **[Neil Pryde](https://www.neilprydesails.com/):** Performance sails.
