# V15 Class Membership

**By V15 Class Association**

> [!NOTE]
> **Historical Note:** The specific dues and forms mentioned in older versions of this page (e.g., $15.00 in 2010) may no longer be accurate. Please contact current [Class Officers](/articles/v15-national-class-officers) or your local fleet captain for the latest membership details.

## Benefits of Membership

Joining the Class Association supports the ongoing health and growth of the Vanguard 15 fleet. Your dues contribute to:

*   **Regatta Support:** Organization and promotion of National and Regional Championships.
*   **Class Communication:** Maintenance of the website, forums, and fleet lists.
*   **Technical Standards:** Ensuring the one-design nature of the boat is preserved through the Technical Committee.
*   **Fleet Development:** Resources to help new and existing fleets grow.

## How to Join

To join the class or renew your membership, please reach out to the Class Treasurer or Secretary. Active membership is often required to participate in sanctioned regattas like the National Championship.

*Support the class that supports your sailing!*
